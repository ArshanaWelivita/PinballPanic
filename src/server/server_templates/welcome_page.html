Welcome to Pinball Panic!

How to Play:

  • The game consists of 16 levels.
  • Your goal is to correctly identify the coordinates of the ball's exit point.
  • Determine the path of the ball using the grid objects.
  • Enter the coordinates in the input fields and submit your answer.
  • As you continue to answer correctly, the levels will increase in difficulty and introduce more types of grid objects.
  • Good luck and have fun!
