Game Rules

Gameplay Overview

The objective PinballPanic is to guess the correct ending position of a ball as it navigates through obstacles.

  • Each level features a grid with various objects that will guide the path of the ball.
  • The levels will increase in difficulty, both in the number of objects and the size of the grid.

How to Play

You have 30 seconds to complete as many levels as possible.

Click on the grid square to submit your answer.

Correct answers add 5 seconds to your timer.

Incorrect answers remove 5 seconds from your timer.
